## Technical Usage Scenarios

When implementing an Instagram account management system, developers often face the challenge of efficiently nurturing new accounts while ensuring compliance with platform guidelines. Building a trustable account profile involves simulating organic activity, which helps to prevent account bans and enhances engagement rates. In this scenario, an automation tool is essential for managing user interactions, maintaining consistent activity levels, and optimizing content delivery.

## How MadHub Fits into This Scenario

MadHub serves as a comprehensive solution for managing Instagram accounts through its automation features. Specifically, the **Instagram Account Warm-Up** feature allows developers to simulate real user behavior, effectively browsing through feeds and engaging with content without being flagged by Instagram’s algorithm. This feature is vital for ensuring that new accounts gain trust and visibility, leading to improved engagement and follower growth.

## Technical Workflow Steps

1. **Set Up MadHub**:
   - Install MadHub on your preferred platform (real Android device, cloud phone, or emulator).
   - Launch the MadHub application and log in with your Instagram account credentials.

2. **Configure Instagram Account Warm-Up**:
   - Navigate to the **Instagram Account Warm-Up** section within MadHub.
   - **Set Interaction Probability**: Define how often the account should engage with content (likes, comments).
   - **Execution Probability Distribution**: Adjust settings for varying levels of engagement during warm-up to mimic realistic user behavior.
   - **Browse Modes**: Choose between homepage and video page browsing depending on the desired engagement strategy.

3. **Initiate Warm-Up Process**:
   - Start the warm-up process, allowing MadHub to automatically simulate user interactions.
   - Monitor real-time analytics provided by MadHub for insights on account performance.
